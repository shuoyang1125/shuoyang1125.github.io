Shuo Yang received his B.E. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering at Southeast University in 2024, now he is pursuing his Ph.D. degree from the School of Electronics at Peking University. 

Shuo Yang is now advised by [Prof. Hongbin Li](https://ele.pku.edu.cn/info/1023/1121.htm) and also co-advised by [Prof. Chuanchuan Yang](https://ele.pku.edu.cn/info/1023/1215.htm). His research interests includes efficient parallel training of large language model (LLM), remote direct memory access (RDMA), network optimization.
